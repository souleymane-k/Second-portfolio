Know Your Numbers

This project consists of helping diabetes patients to input their data in an application that will output whether or not their glycemic level is normal or not. There will be three groups of people such as normal glycemic level, prediabetic group, and diabetes group. The output can show a descriptive graph with different colors. For the normal level of blood glycemia, the app will show the result in the integer number and the corresponding color, which will be green. The color yellow will be affected by prediabetes value and finally red for diabetic output. At least two tests will be performed every day: one before eating and the second one after eating.
